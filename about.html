Founder & Lead Consultant

Dr. Mary Sanders

Dr. Mary enthusiastically shares a continuing expansive body of knowledge to meet the organizational needs from her work consulting with Fortune 500 companies — creating effective systems and cultures with innovative approaches and strategies of change management.

Her research and professional interests are about how to create sustainable and lasting change in organizations through innovative neurolinguistics strategies.

As a Neuro Linguistic Programming Trainer and Master Practitioner, she has integrated this knowledge and these skills into her organizational change work with positive outcomes for all stakeholders.

During her Ph.D. research, she found significant effects of using two NLP methods to reduce resistance to change in a large organization. The two major areas of her work today are examining the effects of AI on critical thinking and how to use NLP language patterns to create positive change during chaotic times — to guide leaders and employees to collectively move forward.

Dr. Sanders has been teaching and training adults about leadership skills throughout her career. She created and led a corporate sales training department using competency models and NLP sales methods in customized workshops for a national healthcare company. She has facilitated thousands of workshops in many industries and in government.

Makrina Feagins

Data Science Consultant & AI Strategist

Makrina Feagins helps organizations turn the complexity of artificial intelligence into measurable results. As a data science consultant specializing in AI algorithms and applications, she partners with corporate leaders and educational institutions to design data-driven solutions that drive smarter decisions, streamline operations, and prepare teams for the realities of an AI-powered future.

What sets Makrina apart is the rare combination of deep technical fluency and over 25 years in the classroom. She doesn't just build models — she translates them. Whether working with a C-suite navigating an enterprise AI initiative or a school district integrating data practices across campuses, Makrina bridges the gap between sophisticated technology and the people who need to use it.

Her foundation is rigorous: a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering and a master's degree in interdisciplinary studies with a concentration in mathematics. She completed advanced coursework in statistics and data science at the University of Texas at San Antonio and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Data Science at National University — ensuring her clients always benefit from current, research-grounded expertise.

Makrina's work is built on applied probability, statistics, and a practitioner's instinct for real-world data. For organizations ready to move beyond the AI conversation and into action, she delivers the clarity and capability to make it happen.
